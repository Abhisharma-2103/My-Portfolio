The best movies according to Abhishek

My top three movies of all time

Blade Runner 2049

This movie has one of the best endings. The ending makes you feel bad about the protagonist's fate. The cinematography and lighting used in this movie was award-winning and it helped the viewers in engaging with the artistic tone of the movie.

Drive

The noir setting of this movie really makes you engage with the plot. The soundtracks used in this movie were no less than perfect. The movie focuses on the relationship between the protagonist, a mother and her child and then how he manages to save them after they are caught in a feud involving crimelords. It also has a meaningful ending.

La L a Land

This movies revolves around two love birds who are struggling with their careers. It beatifully depicts the struggle that modern couples go through when they have to choose between their relationship or their career.
